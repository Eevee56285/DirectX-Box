## Setup Instructions

### 1. Install DirectX‑Box
Begin by installing **directx-box**.

### 2. Create Your Account
When prompted during setup:
- Set the **account name** to `xbox`
- Enable **auto‑login**

### 3. First Launch of Ubuntu
After Ubuntu finishes setting up and you reach the desktop, continue below.

### 4. Add Xemu to Startup Applications
1. Open **Startup Applications**
2. Click **Add**
3. Enter the following:

**Name:** `Xemu`  
**Command:** `xemu`  
**Comment:** *(anything you want)*
